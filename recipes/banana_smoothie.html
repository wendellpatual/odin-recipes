Banana Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 banana

  • 1 cup of milk (cow's milk, almond milk, coconut milk, etc.)

  • 1/2 cup of yogurt (plain, vanilla, or flavored)

  • 1 teaspoon of honey (or other sweetener)

  • A few ice cubes (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all the necessary ingredients: 1 banana, 1 cup of milk, 1/2 cup of yogurt, 1 teaspoon of honey, and a few ice cubes (optional).
  2. Peel the banana and cut it into small pieces.
  3. Add the banana, milk, yogurt, and honey to a blender and blend until smooth.
  4. If you prefer a thicker smoothie, add a few ice cubes and blend again.
  5. Pour the smoothie into a glass and enjoy immediately.
