Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Garam masala
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Heavy cream

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in a mixture of yogurt, lemon juice, and a blend of Indian spices such as cumin, coriander, and garam masala.
  2. Grill or broil the chicken until it is cooked through.
  3. In a separate pan, make the tomato cream sauce by sautéing onions, garlic, and ginger.
  4. Add canned tomatoes, heavy cream, and a blend of Indian spices to the pan with the onions, garlic, and ginger.
  5. Add the cooked chicken to the sauce and simmer everything together until the sauce has thickened and the chicken is fully coated.
  6. Serve over rice or with naan bread.
